# Code Enforcement Management System – Retrospective Business Analysis

## Overview:

This repository documents a retrospective business analysis project for a technology solution designed to support a code enforcement agency used by multiple different state and municipal agencies. The agency oversees enforcement across multiple domains, including zoning and housing, operational and business licensing, and environmental compliance.

Delivered as a collaborative effort within a business analyst training cohort, this project highlights applied BA techniques in requirements analysis, visual modeling, and cross-functional communication between business stakeholders and technical teams.

## My Contributions

- Requirements Elicitation: Gathered and refined business requirements to define the scope and objectives of the solution.
- Process Modeling & Technical Communication: Designed process flow and modeling diagrams to bridge business needs and technical implementation, ensuring shared understanding across teams.
- Requirements Translation: Converted business requirements into actionable functional, non-functional, and technical requirements.
- Stakeholder Management: Coordinated across the BA team to align priorities, manage deliverables, and maintain consistent documentation standards.
- Documentation: Produced comprehensive project artifacts to support traceability and stakeholder alignment.

## Tools Used

- MicroSoft Word and Excel - Requirements documentation, matrices, and prioritization
- Figma - Wireframes and prototypes
- Draw.io - Process flow diagrams and visual modeling
